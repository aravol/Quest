# Kelvin, the guide
The pilot and guide of [Silence](Silence), Kelvin is a four-armed [Sahuaguin](../Races/Sahuaguin) who, facing discrimination for his mutations, left his people to work for [Art Garfunckle](./ArtGarfunckle).
